8 Days & 7 Nights Kenya Classic Safari Package

Magical Kenya has alot to offer with our great safari packages, Get to enjoy 8 days of thrilling and adventurous tour in our carefully selected 5 destinations - Samburu, Aberdares, Nakuru, Navasha & Masai Mara.

Day 1

Nairobi Overnight

Pick up from the airport by our driver guide and transferred to the hotel for overnight – Ibis Styles Hotel – Meal Plan –Bed Breakfast

Day 2

Nairobi /Samburu National Reserve

After breakfast our driver guide will pick you from the hotel at 0730hrs with small briefing and drive north to Samburu National Reserve to arrive in time for lunch at the reserved lodge. After lunch proceed for an afternoon game drive as from 1600hrs until dusk. Samburu Game Reserve offers a wide range of attractions. It is home to the Samburu tribe who are also pastoralists. Ewasso Nyiro River which runs along the southern boundary is home to crocodile and hippo with the many animals visiting to drink during the heat of the day. In Samburu you are likely to spot buffalo, leopard, lion, cheetah, Grevy's zebra, Beisa Oryx, reticulated giraffe and other plains game- Dinner and overnight at Samburu Simba Lodge –Meal Plan –Full Board

Day 3

Full Day in Samburu National Reserve/ Aberdare National Park.

Early morning game drive, then you break for breakfast at your camp. After breakfast leave Samburu for Aberdare a 4 Hrs drive arriving with a short game en-route check in at Aberdare Country Club, have lunch and relax. Afternoon game drive across s Aberdares park Later dinner and overnight at Aberdare Country Club-Meal Plan- Full Board

Day 4

Aberdare - Lake Nakuru/ Lake Naivasha

Early morning breakfast game en-route leave Aberdare for Lake Nakuru a less than 5 Hrs drive and pass through the Great Rift Valley escarpment, arriving in time for lunch and at Sarova lion hill Lodge. Afternoon game drive across the Pink Lake often referred so due to it’s Great masses of Flamingos not forgetting the famous white Rhino and black rhino found in this park. Depart to Lake Naivasha for a late check in followed by dinner and overnight at Lake Nakuru Sopa Lodge –Meal Plan –Full Board

Day 5

Lake Naivasha - Masai Mara

Early morning breakfast. Followed by a boat ride in Lake Naivasha .Depart and leave Lake Naivasha for Masai Mara A 5 hours drive. You will pass to Narok town which is famous Masai town proceeding to Masai Mara park. you will arrive for late lunch Check in have lunch. Afternoon game drive through the park in search of the Lion, Cheetah, Elephant, Buffalo and other members of the Big five plus other animals. Dinner and overnight at Mara Sopa Lodge –Meal Plan -Full Board.

Day 6

Masai Mara full day

Early morning game drive and return to camp for breakfast. After breakfast Full day in the park with packed lunch in search of its popular residents, The Masai Mara plains are full of wildebeest during migration season early July to end of September, zebra, impala, topi, giraffe, Thomson's gazelle are regularly seen, leopards, lions, hyenas, cheetah, jackal and bat-eared foxes. Black rhino are a little shy and hard to spot but are often seen at a distance if you are lucky. Hippos are abundant in the Mara River as are very large Nile crocodiles, who lay in wait for a meal as the wildebeest cross on their annual quest to find new pastures. later Meals and overnight at Mara Sopa Lodge –Meal Plan -Full Board.

Day 7

Masai Mara - Nairobi

Early Morning breakfast at your camp. check out of the camp and Drive to Nairobi A 5 hours drive to Nairobi, arriving in time for lunch. Lunch at Carnivore afterwards drop off at your respective hotel or Airport around 3pm. ( Optional to our clients with evening Flights) - if you have evening flight you can do more game drive with packed lunch till around 12:00hrs lunch time, After you drive to Nairobi you arrive in Nairobi at around 5 to 6 pm drop off at Airport or back to your hotel.
