Group Joining Safari | 3 Days & 2 Nights

Discounted & affordable nonresident group joining rates to Maasai Mara for 3 days and 2 nights starting from USD 150 per person sharing.

PROPOSED ITINERARY

Day 1

You shall be picked up from Nairobi CBD depart around 7.30 am and go to the escarpment descending further down into the plains of the great rift valley, take a short break in Narok town and head to the world famous Masai Mara. Check in for lunch. Relax and later head for game drive at 4.00pm, return around 6.30pm to lodge. – Dinner and night at Camp/Lodge on full board basis.

Day 2

Rise up for breakfast and later head out for game drive armed with picnic lunch boxes for a full day game drive hence able to explore more areas of Masai Mara as opposed to going and back to camp for lunch. Go visit known hippo pools and Mara river and of cause, search for game. Enjoy your lunch boxes while at the river or under known picnic site under solitary "Olkeju" trees. Return back to lodge around 4 - 5pm - Dinner and night at Camp/Lodge on full board basis.

Day 3

After breakfast, depart around 9.30am and stop at Narok town, have snacks/ lunch (payment direct) and proceed to the escarpment to arrival in Nairobi in the afternoon. You shall be dropped in the CBD

End of safari!!
